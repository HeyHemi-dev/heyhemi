# Vision

## **Mayday Weddings. Let Help Find You.Fast.**

Are you a wedding vendor looking for someone to help you cover a New Zealand wedding?

Simply post a job request, and let us bring local & available talent to you.

## **How It Works**

1. **Post a job to the Job Board**

   You will have the opportunity to define job details such as the date, location, est budget, skills, and hours needed. As well as choose if the job request is urgent priority.

2. **We will message local vendors**

   We will email local vendors who match the job details. They can respond with just one click, to indicate they are available.

3. **Browse the list of creatives**

   You get a link to the job page with a list of available vendors, who you can view and filter based on skill level, relative price, and more.

## Mayday vs. Facebook

Why Mayday is better than Facebook Groups for finding local wedding vendors fast.

### **Get Seen**

Facebook groups are a catch-all for everything, so important conversations quickly get buried in the feed.

Mayday is super focused on finding you help, so you can be sure your request will not be missed.

### **Reach Creatives**

Facebook usage is sliding as people limit their time on social media. Additionally 100's of creatives —your potential help —are not in the wedding-specific groups.

Mayday job posts are delivered to creative's inboxes where they won't be missed. Urgent job posts are also sent via SMS to ensure you get responses fast!

### **Find the Right Person**

On Facebook, potential help is a mixture of comments, DMs and people tagging other people —which means extra work for finding the right creative for your job.

Mayday makes it easy to find the right creative; responses are formatted as a list you can view and filter based on skill level, relative price, preferred camera brand and more.

## **Tech**

I built Mayday by connecting low-code platforms together; Webflow for front end, Airtable for data, Make for backend, Sendgrid for emails, and some JS for on-page logic.

After launch I continued to improve the app; troubleshoot problems such as rate limits, email deliverability, error handling, as well as adding features such as updating profiles (with a review process), job filtering, on-page add to job, and more.

Mayday Weddings was a mission-critical web application designed to solve an urgent problem in the wedding industry during the COVID-19 pandemic. The challenge was to create a rapid-response system that could quickly connect wedding vendors who needed to isolate with qualified replacements, ensuring that couples' special days could proceed smoothly despite last-minute vendor unavailability.

The solution was a streamlined job board platform that automated the entire replacement process. By integrating low-code solutions like Webflow, Airtable, Make, and SendGrid, I created a system that could instantly match and notify qualified vendors, facilitate one-click responses, and present a filtered list of available candidates complete with ratings and experience levels. The platform's success was evident in its impact: connecting 500 vendors, saving 100 weddings, and garnering significant media attention from major news outlets.

Post-launch improvements focused on scalability and user experience, including enhanced profile management, advanced job filtering, and robust error handling. The project demonstrates how thoughtful technical architecture and user-centered design can come together to solve real-world problems effectively.
